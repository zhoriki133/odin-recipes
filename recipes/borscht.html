Borscht

Description

This classic dish is a made with plenty of wholesome ingredients, like potatoes, beef and carrots. It's best served hot with sour cream and some sliced bread.

I find it to be the perfect dish on a cold winter day to sit and enjoy while watching the snow fall. Filled with meat and potatoes you are sure to be full and satisfied after this meal!

Ingredients

  • Beet
  • Potato
  • Beef, chicken, or meat of choice
  • Carrot
  • Cabbage
  • Dill
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Broth, either home made or premade

Steps

  1. Prep the meat, then brown the beef, and cook down the onions
  2. Peel and slice your potatoes
  3. Add your broth, simmer until beef is tender
  4. Remove and clean the beef of fat and bone and chop into pieces
  5. Skim the fat off the broth
  6. Add vegetables to the broth
  7. Add the meat back into the broth
  8. Add the cabbage and meat into the broth
  9. Simmer until cabbage is cooked through
  10. Top with dill and season to taste
  11. Serve into a bowl, top with sour cream
