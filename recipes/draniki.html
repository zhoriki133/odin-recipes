Draniki "Potato Pancakes"

Description

These are delicious, crispy, fried potatoes in a fun way! You can make hashbrowns, french fries, or you could make draniki! They are soft and fluffy potato on the inside, and salty, crispy deliciousness on the outside.

We like to eat these with a dollop of sour cream on top, just for the extra savory flavor! They are just too good, so be sure to make plenty because they will be gone before you know it!

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sour cream
  • Sunflower oil (any cooking oil will do)

Steps

  1. Wash, peel and shred your potatoes
  2. Mix eggs and flour into a mixing bowl
  3. Heat up oil in a pan
  4. Mix the shreded potatoes into the egg and flour mixture
  5. Take a spoonfull of the potato mix and place it into the oil
  6. Pat it down gently and add more if you want it thicker
  7. Cook until firm on one side, then flip
  8. When golden brown remove from the oil
  9. Place onto paper towl to remove excess oil, then plate
  10. Serve as a side dish or on it's own with a spoonful of sour cream
