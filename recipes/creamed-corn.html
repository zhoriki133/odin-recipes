Creamed Corn Like No Other!

Description

This is one appetizing side dish! When you think of something salty, sweet and savory, this is what you should think of! The corn kernels burst with flavor, enveloped in a delicious creamy cheesy sauce.

When you need the perfect side dish for your classic meat and potatoes, just to add some flavor and flare. This is the dish to make! It's super easy, the ingredients are very simple, and it just tastes great.

Ingredients

  • Sweet corn (canned is fine)
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • All purpose flour
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Set a skillet on the stove over medium heat
  2. Combine the corn, cream, salt, sugar, pepper and butter in the skillet
  3. Whisk together the milk and flour in a bowl
  4. Slowly stir in the milk and flour mix into the skillet
  5. Cook while stirring over medium heat until the mixture thickens (this will take some time)
  6. Remove from the heat, stir in parmesan cheese until completely melted
  7. Serve hot, and enjoy!
